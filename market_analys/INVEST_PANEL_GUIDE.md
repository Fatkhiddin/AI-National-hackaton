- Bozor narxlari bazasi (MarketPriceReference)
- Jami tahlillar soni
- CRM property tahlillari
- OLX property tahlillari
- O'rtacha confidence score
- Arzon OLX takliflar

### 📈 Bo'limlar
- Qurilish turi bo'yicha
- Holat bo'yicha (remontli/remontsiz)
- Status bo'yicha (arzon, normal, qimmat)
